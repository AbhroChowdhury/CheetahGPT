Welcome to MultiServe.AI

MultiServe.AI lets you leverage AI on a variety of structured and unstructured data sources such as YouTube, Reddit, CSV's, and more!

We all know of ChatGPT by now, however one of its biggest limitations is the scope of its training data. Say you're a student who wants to see what other students think about a certain professor; Unfortunately, ChatGPT will not be able to help you with that. Reddit-GPT on the other hand, can! By parsing through posts of your universities subreddit and temporarily adding it to the existing training data, Reddit-GPT can quickly answer high-level questions such as the aforementioned.
