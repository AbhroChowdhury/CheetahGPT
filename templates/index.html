Welcome to CheetahGPT

CheetahGPT lets you leverage AI on a variety of structured and unstructured data sources such as YouTube, Reddit, CSV's, and more!

Reddit-GPT

Pick a subreddit, some keywords, and Reddit-GPT will add all posts within that criteria to its knowledge base. Scrolling through countless replies and posts to find the answer you're looking for is now a thing of the past!

Youtube-GPT

Paste the link of any video you want; Youtube-GPT will create a transcript of it, and add it to its knowledge base. Want to clarify something you saw in a video, or gain further insights on it? Look no further!

Wiki-GPT

Pick some keywords, and Wiki-GPT will find those subsequent wikiepdia pages and add them to its knowledge base. Want to learn about something recent that happened in the past two years? Wiki-GPT will be able to help you!

And More!

Have an Excel file/CSV that you'd like to use AI on? Or what about a document? CheetahGPT has the functionality to let you work with both! We are constantly working to add new data connections; Stay tuned for new releases!
