Welcome to MultiServe.AI

MultiServe.AI lets you leverage AI on a variety of structured and unstructured data sources such as YouTube, Reddit, CSV's, and more!

We all know of ChatGPT by now, however one of its biggest limitations is the scope of its training data. While the sheer amount of training data that ChatGPT was built on is incredibly large, it does not include personal files/datasets. Neither does it include new youtube videos, new reddit posts, new wikipedia pages, or anything that is being constantly updated. MultiServe.AI is here to precisely fill in that gap of knowledge; By giving AI modles access to specific data related to your situation, you can leverage the power of AI to maximize your efficiency and get insights quicker.
